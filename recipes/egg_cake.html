Egg Cake

Description:

Desserts need to tick three very simple boxes for the healthy individual: Easy to make, protein-filled, and few ingredients.

The egg cake achieves all three, as it only uses one ingredient and has plenty of protein to fill anyone up. Not only that, but it's just so easy to make!

Ingredients:

  • Eggs

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400F.
  2. Place the eggs in a baking bowl as shown below.
  3. Place baking bowl into the oven and let it bake for 10 minutes.
  4. Once it's done, remove from oven and let sit for 3 minutes.
  5. Enjoy!
