Grass Bread

Description:

Did your neighbor just mow their lawn and now the grass clippings are all over your car? Do you hate how your driveway is now a different color, and there's just nothing you can do about all the green litering your yard?

Worry not, for we've got the perfect solution to your morning-dew-smelling woes. This recipe will allow you to take all those wasted grass clippings and turn them into a yummy dessert you can serve at the dinner table!

Ingredients:

  • Grass(shredded)
  • Flour (for taste)

Now, let's get into the nitty gritty of making a delicious serving of grass bread.

Instructions:

  1. Place all the shredded grass on a baking tray.
  2. With your hands or tool of your choice, press all of the bread into a rectangle. You can make it as tall or as short as you want.
  3. To allow the inside to breathe, grab a butter knife and slice some shallow lines about an inch from each other atop the loaf.
  4. Sprinkle flour by hand over bread to taste.
