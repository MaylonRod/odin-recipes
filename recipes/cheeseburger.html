Cheeseburger
Description:

A twist on this American classic, here we've got Big Poppa's Big Burger. It's everything that makes a cheeseburger special, only with much more of it.

So, let's get started on this recipe.

Ingredients:
  • 200$ worth of American Cheese slices. Lots of them.
  • Burger Buns
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Onions
  • Pickles
  • Burger
Directions:
  1. Assemble the burger buns.
  2. Place the burger on the bottom bun
  3. Stack the cheese slices until you run out. Make sure to keep balance.
  4. Top off with the mustard, onions, pickles, and ketchup.
  5. Enjoy!
